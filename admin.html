Recommended workflow

  • Open Bookings and confirm the customer job.
  • Assign the detailer or senior detailer.
  • Enable the customer progress link if needed.
  • Open Job Site and complete the pre-inspection intake.
  • Use Live Monitor to follow the job in real time.
  • Use Progress to review customer-facing notes, media, and signoffs.
  • Let the customer review and sign off after completion.

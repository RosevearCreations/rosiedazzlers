Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Rosie Dazzlers Mobile Auto Detailing respects your privacy and aims to handle personal information responsibly. This page explains the kinds of information we may collect through our website and booking process, how we use it, and how you can contact us with privacy questions.

1. Information we may collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Service address
  • Vehicle size and booking details
  • Add-on selections and appointment notes
  • Technical information needed for website operation and booking security

2. Why we collect it

We collect information primarily to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Process and manage bookings
  • Collect deposits and confirm appointments
  • Communicate about services, scheduling, and changes
  • Improve website functionality and customer experience

3. Payments

Deposit payments may be processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe. Rosie Dazzlers does not store full payment card information on this website. Payment providers handle payment data according to their own terms and privacy practices.

4. Website tools and third parties

The site may use third-party services to operate booking, hosting, analytics, forms, databases, email, or payments. These may include Cloudflare, GitHub, Supabase, Stripe, and other service providers used to run the website and booking system.

5. Marketing communications

If Rosie Dazzlers sends promotional emails or similar commercial electronic messages, those communications should only be sent where appropriate consent exists and should include sender identification and an unsubscribe method.

6. Data retention

We may retain customer and booking information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the business, maintain records, manage appointments, resolve disputes, and meet legal, accounting, or operational needs.

7. Access and correction

If you want to ask what personal information we hold about you, or request corrections to inaccurate information, contact us using the email below.

8. Security

We use reasonable steps to protect personal information connected to our website and booking process. However, no website, email system, or online platform can guarantee absolute security.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact:
info@rosiedazzlers.ca

This privacy page is a practical website policy, not legal advice. If you add newsletters, SMS marketing, paid ads/remarketing, or extensive analytics, update this policy.

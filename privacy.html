Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Rosie Dazzlers Mobile Auto Detailing collects, uses, and protects your information.

Bookings Payments Photos

1) Information we collect

  • Contact information: name, email, phone.
  • Service location: address for mobile service.
  • Vehicle information: year/make/model, size selection, optional photos provided by customer.
  • Booking details: service date/time, selected package, add-ons.
  • Payment information: processed by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Technical data: basic logs (device/browser, IP) for security and troubleshooting.

2) How we use your information

  • To schedule and deliver mobile detailing services.
  • To communicate about appointments, changes, and service updates.
  • To process deposits and payments.
  • To improve service quality and resolve disputes (including keeping private before/after documentation).

3) Photos and media

  • We may take before/after photos for service records.
  • We will not post your identifiable vehicle/location publicly without permission.
  • If you do not want photos used for marketing, tell us in writing.

4) Sharing information

We only share information when necessary to operate the service:

  • Stripe: processes your payment.
  • Supabase: stores booking records and operational data.
  • Cloudflare: hosts the website and provides security/performance services.

5) Data retention

  • We retain booking records for business operations and accounting.
  • We may retain service photos as private documentation unless you request removal (where feasible).

6) Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data where applicable.
  • To make a request, contact us at info@rosiedazzlers.ca.

7) Security

We use reputable service providers and standard security practices. No online system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

Note: This page is general information and not legal advice. For a lawyer-reviewed privacy policy for Ontario/Canada, consider professional review.

Why this page exists

These special-service pages are meant to stand on their own. They give the service enough room for process, equipment, expectations, proof, and clear reasons to book or ask for a quote.

Ceramic coating is only as good as the prep underneath it. Paint condition, wash history, contamination, and correction needs all affect the final result and the real quote.

Process and preparation

  • wash and decontamination planning before the coating step
  • paint condition review to decide whether correction is needed first
  • surface prep and protection recommendations based on the vehicle finish
  • quote-first workflow so the coating package matches the actual vehicle

A dedicated service page should look like a real service page. That means process, visuals, equipment context, and clear expectations instead of a thin placeholder.

Equipment and workflow

  • surface prep products for contamination removal
  • inspection lighting for paint-condition review
  • applicator workflow built for controlled coating application

Common questions

Is ceramic coating a same-day quick add-on?

Usually no. The prep work matters, so the vehicle condition has to be reviewed first.

Do you need exterior detailing first?

Yes. The paint needs to be properly cleaned and prepared before coating is considered.

Why is this usually quote required?

Because paint condition, contamination, and needed prep time can change the real scope of work.

Customer Waiver

This waiver explains what we need on-site and what you agree to when booking Rosie Dazzlers Mobile Auto Detailing.

Driveway required Power + water required Local bylaws

1) Service location requirements

  • Driveway required. Service is performed on your driveway or a safe, approved private surface.
  • Power + water required. You must provide access to an outdoor water source and a working power outlet.
  • If power/water are not available (or not usable), additional charges may apply or the appointment may need to be rescheduled.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service if the work area is unsafe (ice, traffic hazard, aggressive pets, etc.).

2) Environmental / bylaw responsibility

  • Mobile detailing may involve soaps, cleaners, and runoff.
  • You confirm you have permission to have vehicle cleaning performed at the service address.
  • You accept responsibility for any municipal or property bylaws, restrictions, HOA rules, or notices related to washing/chemicals/runoff at your location.
  • If you want “waterless-only” or “minimal runoff” methods, tell us before booking so we can confirm feasibility.

3) Vehicle condition & limitations

  • We work carefully, but some results depend on vehicle condition (stains, scratches, etching, worn coatings, heavy pet hair, smoke odour, etc.).
  • We are not responsible for pre-existing issues (paint failure, cracked leather, weak trim clips, failing electronics, brittle plastics, etc.).
  • If we identify a risk (e.g., damaged clear coat), we may adjust the process or recommend an alternative approach.

4) Photos & documentation

  • We may take before/after photos for quality documentation and customer reference.
  • If you do not want photos used publicly, tell us in writing (we will still keep private documentation if needed for service records).

5) Scheduling, deposits, and cancellations

  • We typically book one vehicle per day unless two half-day services are scheduled.
  • A deposit is required to reserve your time.
  • Cancellation fees may apply (details are shown during booking and in your confirmation).
  • Weather or unsafe conditions may require rescheduling. We will coordinate a new date/time with you.
Important: This page provides general service terms and is not legal advice. If you need a lawyer-reviewed waiver for your business, we recommend having one drafted/reviewed for Ontario.

Mobile Service Waiver

Last updated: March 6, 2026

By proceeding with a booking, the customer acknowledges and agrees to the following service conditions for mobile auto detailing.

1. Service location

The customer confirms that a suitable driveway, parking area, or work space is available for service and that the location is safe and reasonably accessible.

2. Power and water

The customer confirms that access to power and water is available unless other arrangements have been agreed to in advance. If required resources are not available on arrival, additional fees, reduced scope, rescheduling, or cancellation may apply.

3. Bylaws and property rules

The customer accepts responsibility for ensuring that the service location allows detailing activity, including any rules relating to runoff, water use, chemical use, property management, landlord restrictions, condominium rules, or municipal bylaws.

4. Vehicle condition

The customer understands that service outcomes vary depending on the age, material, maintenance history, and condition of the vehicle. Some defects, stains, odors, scratches, or damage may not be fully removable.

5. Weather and interruption

Because service is mobile, appointments may need to be delayed, interrupted, or rescheduled due to unsafe weather or site conditions.

6. Personal property

Customers should remove valuables, important documents, cash, fragile items, and personal belongings before service begins. Rosie Dazzlers is not responsible for items left in the vehicle unless required by law.

7. Access and readiness

The customer agrees to ensure that the vehicle is accessible at the agreed time and that keys or access arrangements are available where needed. Missed access may result in cancellation fees or loss of deposit.

8. Booking acknowledgement

By checking the required acknowledgements during booking and proceeding with payment, the customer confirms acceptance of these service conditions.

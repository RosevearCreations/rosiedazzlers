    Upload guidance

    Use the front and rear outline guides below to keep the whole vehicle in frame. Photo uploads are validated locally before upload and checked again at save time using the merchandising score. Videos still stay available for manual review.

    Front angle
    Stand centered, keep the bumper and both headlights in frame, and leave a little space around the vehicle.
    Rear angle
    Stand centered behind the vehicle and keep both taillights, bumper, and trunk or hatch fully visible.
    • General image minimum: 800×800 and square or landscape.
    • First image target: at least 1200×1200, square or landscape, with stronger alt-text checks.
    • Upload-time analysis now flags dark, blurry, cluttered, or duplicate-looking angles before save.
    • Short videos are still allowed for walkarounds or damage notes.

Why this page exists

These special-service pages are meant to stand on their own. They give the service enough room for process, equipment, expectations, proof, and clear reasons to book or ask for a quote.

Headlight restoration is about clarity, appearance, and safer-looking forward lighting. The amount of oxidation and lens condition changes the real work required.

Process and preparation

  • inspection of lens cloudiness and oxidation level
  • cleaning and prep before restoration work starts
  • restoration steps matched to the condition of the lens surface
  • finish review so the customer can see the improvement clearly

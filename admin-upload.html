Upload Progress Photo

Upload a photo privately (Supabase Storage), then attach it to the customer progress feed.

Uses /api/admin/progress_upload_url (signed upload) and stores media as sb://bucket/path.

Upload

Tip: take a photo on your phone and upload it here.

How it works

  1. Request a signed upload URL (private upload, no public bucket).
  2. Upload the file directly to Supabase Storage.
  3. Save a progress photo row referencing sb://bucket/path.
  4. Customer page signs a temporary view link automatically.

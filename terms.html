Estimated service times are approximate. Actual time may vary based on vehicle condition, size, weather, access, safety concerns, and add-on services. Rosie Dazzlers generally limits service to one vehicle per day unless the booking is suitable for a half-day schedule.

Pricing and package recommendations are based on typical vehicle condition. Excessive soil, heavy pet hair, severe stains, biohazards, mold, excessive debris, or unusual contamination may require additional charges, reduced scope, or refusal of service.

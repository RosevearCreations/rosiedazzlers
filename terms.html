1) Service area

We provide mobile auto detailing services within Norfolk and Oxford Counties, Ontario. Additional travel fees may apply outside our usual range.

2) Booking and deposits

  • Bookings are reserved by paying a deposit during online checkout.
  • Deposits secure your appointment time. Remaining balance is due at service or per invoice/payment method.
  • We typically service one vehicle per day, unless two half-day jobs are scheduled.

3) Cancellations and rescheduling

  • Cancellations may be subject to a fee depending on timing and preparation already performed.
  • If weather or unsafe conditions prevent service, we will reschedule with you.

  • Driveway required.
  • Customer provides power and water. If not available, additional charges may apply or service may be rescheduled.

7) Promo codes

  • Promo codes may be time-limited, usage-limited, or restricted.
  • Promo codes cannot be guaranteed and may be disabled at any time.
  • Promo codes are applied at checkout and validated by our system.

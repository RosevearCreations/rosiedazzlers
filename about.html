About Rosie Dazzlers

Rosie Dazzlers Mobile Auto Detailing is the brainchild of our children and grew into a service built around convenience, quality, and care. We created Rosie Dazzlers to bring professional mobile auto detailing directly to our customers, making it easier to keep vehicles clean, protected, and looking their best without the hassle of leaving home or work.

Serving Norfolk and Oxford Counties, Rosie Dazzlers is more than just a detailing service. It is a commitment to careful work, clear expectations, and a more eco-conscious approach to vehicle care. We focus on meticulous attention to detail and use products and methods chosen to help protect both your vehicle and the environment whenever possible.

Mobile detailing gives customers the full detailing experience at their own location. That means personalized service, flexible scheduling, and the convenience of having the work done where your vehicle already is. For many people, that saves time, reduces stress, and makes regular vehicle care much easier to fit into daily life.

What we do
Mobile auto detailing with packages for exterior, interior, and full detail services.
Where we serve
Norfolk County and Oxford County, with service brought directly to the customer.
How we work
Deposit-secured bookings, clear package pricing, add-ons, and one vehicle per day in most cases.

What mobile detailing includes

  • Exterior washing and drying — hand washing the vehicle exterior, wheels, and tires to remove dirt and grime.
  • Waxing and polishing — adding shine and helping protect paint, with polishing available for light swirls and defects.
  • Interior cleaning — vacuuming seats, carpets, mats, and trunk, plus wiping down and disinfecting surfaces.
  • Engine bay cleaning — light cleaning to remove dirt and grease for a cleaner appearance.
  • Detailing extras — services such as paint correction, ceramic coatings, headlight restoration, and pet hair removal.

Why people choose mobile detailing

  • Convenience — no need to drop off your vehicle and wait around.
  • Personalized service — more attention is given to your vehicle and its specific needs.
  • Flexible scheduling — easier to fit around work, home life, and daily routines.
  • Better products and techniques — professional tools and supplies can provide better results than a quick drive-through wash.

What customers should know before booking

  • A driveway or suitable work area is required.
  • Customers must provide access to power and water, or accept additional charges where applicable.
  • Deposits are required to secure an appointment.
  • Cancellation fees may apply.
  • Customers accept responsibility for any local bylaw or runoff restrictions at their location.
  • We typically limit bookings to one vehicle per day unless the service is a half-day job.

Simple maintenance tips between appointments

  • Wash your vehicle regularly to reduce dirt and grime buildup.
  • Clean spills and debris quickly to avoid stains and odours.
  • Park in the shade where possible to reduce UV exposure.
  • Use regular upkeep between full details to help preserve value and finish.
